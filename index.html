About FedEx

FedEx Corporation is a global courier delivery services company headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee, USA. Founded in 1971, FedEx revolutionized the shipping industry with its overnight delivery service and real-time package tracking. Today, FedEx provides transportation, e-commerce, and business services worldwide, operating in over 220 countries and territories. Known for reliability and speed, FedEx connects people and businesses by delivering millions of packages every day.
